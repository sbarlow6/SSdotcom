Race to the finish!

Sorcery Slam is a competitive/party puzzle game inspired by retro era classics. Play with up to 6 Sorcerers locally or online to collect powerful elemental Gems and be the first to power up your ultimate final blast for the win!

Slam and Stash your way to victory!

Get your block in position and place it instantly with the SLAM button! Save a block or item for a more opportune time with the bank button!

Cast hexes with combos!

Clear more than one line with a single block to clutter your opponents with hexes! Larger combos cause stronger attacks, and complete in real time! Begin your next move immediately with fast paced puzzling action!
